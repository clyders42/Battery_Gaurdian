Core Features (Minimal & Focused)
Primary Function

1% Detection: Monitors battery level specifically for the 1% threshold
Instant Overlay: Immediately shows 2-minute countdown timer over all apps
Persistent Timer: Stays visible until battery dies or gets plugged in
Prominent Display: Large, impossible-to-miss countdown with clear messaging

Essential Settings (Keep It Simple)

Enable/Disable: Toggle the app on/off
Timer Duration: Adjust from 1-5 minutes (default 2 minutes)
Timer Style: Choose between different visual styles (minimal options)
Sound Alert: Optional beep when timer starts

Quality of Life Features

Charger Connected: Timer disappears if you plug in during countdown
Restart Protection: Automatically restarts monitoring after reboot

Core Logic Flow

Service Starts: Battery watcher runs in background (foreground service)
1% Detected: BroadcastReceiver catches battery level change
Overlay Triggers: Countdown timer appears over everything
Timer Runs: 2-minute countdown with prominent display
Completion: Timer ends or disappears when plugged in
